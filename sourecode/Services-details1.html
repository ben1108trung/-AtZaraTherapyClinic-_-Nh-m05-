Massage Therapy

Overall Wellness and Relaxation

Massage therapy can be beneficial for both physical and mental health. Some of the benefits include; stress reduction, pain relief, promoting relaxation, improving sleep, and strengthening the immune system. We provide a high-quality treatment, delivered by a Registered Massage Therapist in a warm and inviting environment - at a price you can afford. Regardless of the reason for your visit - treatment for chronic injury, muscle tension, back, neck, lower body pain or just to help you relax - you will meet with an RMT to review your health history and determine the best treatment plan and technique for you. We offer different types of massage such as but not limited to Swedish massage, Deep tissue massage, Sports massage, Shiatsu, Thai, Lymphatic Drainage, Hot stone, pregnant massage, and Reflexology.

All types of Massage Therapies

Massage Therapy is the assessment and treatment of soft tissue and joint dysfunction and pain. Massage can help restore flexibility and range of motion as well as increase overall wellness and relaxation. Treatment can include myofascial release, thai massage, acupuncture/ electro-acupuncture, therapeutic ultrasound and taping, IFC, joint mobilization, home exercise and cupping therapy
